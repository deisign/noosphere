## Identity

Pilot issue of [[noosphere-concept]]. Title: «Будущее — это дом с привидениями» / English: "The Future Is a Haunted House." Theme: domestic technology as haunted archive.

## Thesis

The smart home is not the fulfillment of a future dream — it is an archive of human traces: voices, habits, routes, absences, and promises that became obsolete before they were fulfilled. Machines imitate care. Rooms learn to remember. Comfort becomes surveillance. Old technologies return as ghosts.

## Main signals

- voice without a body;
- care without presence;
- comfort as surveillance;
- room as interface;
- machine as archive of habits;
- an obsolete promise of the future;
- automation of loneliness.

These map directly to the [[nooscope-machine]] Domestic Haunting Index scores for this issue.

## Fiction layer

Standard scheme (not three full stories per issue):

```text
1 main story
+ 1 object-adjacent fiction material
+ 1 short Reading Room note
+ 1 original microfiction artifact
```

For Issue 001:

- **main story**: Ambrose Bierce — *Moxon's Master*;
- **object-adjacent**: H. G. Wells — *The Crystal Egg*, treated as an object-interface;
- **Reading Room**: Poe — *The Facts in the Case of M. Valdemar*, framed on the "voice after body" line;
- **original artifact**: a smart-home log / Aftercall instruction manual / EchoHome service record.
